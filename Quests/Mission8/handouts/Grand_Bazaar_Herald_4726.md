## HEADLINES

### COUNTERFEIT SILVER PANICS MONEYCHANGERS
**The Coins** — Morning trade ground to a halt today as the "wrong-mint" counterfeit scandal spread through the Grand Bazaar. Accusations of guild sabotage are flying between rival banking houses. Trade Prince Aaqir al'Hakam has publicly advised his partners to rely solely on established letters of credit or Qadiran gold until the Grand Council can verify the silver's origin. The Guild of Wonders is reportedly monitoring the situation closely.

### THASSILONIAN EMBASSY SPARKS REAL ESTATE BOOM
**Foreign Quarter** — Despite the brief, chaotic lockdown at the New Thassilonian Embassy yesterday—involving rumors of magical smoke and vanishing children—the economic impact of their arrival is undeniable. Property values near the embassy have skyrocketed, with merchants eager to secure trade contracts with the ancient nation. Lord Firandivar's refusal to comment on the anomaly hasn't deterred investors, who see Thassilonian gold as the next great opportunity.

### AL-AMIR KAI FACES SILK SHORTAGES
**Ivy District** — High society is in a panic as master tailor Al-Amir Kai reports a temporary shortage of his signature Qadiran silks. The proprietor of the Ready Petal blames recent shipping delays and an unprecedented demand for his gaudy, Padishah-inspired designs. Apprentices are working around the clock to fulfill backorders, but some aristocrats may have to attend this weekend's galas in *last season's* garments. A true tragedy.

### WINGED SANDALS REPORT MASSIVE DELAYS
**Wise Quarter** — Aftrin Undrol, the boastful master conveyor of the Winged Sandals, has issued a rare apology for city-wide message delivery delays. Operating out of the Blue Tower, Undrol claims a sudden influx of mysterious, high-priority missives has overwhelmed his agents. However, critics suggest his operation is simply understaffed, and note that he has been aggressively hiring off-duty adventurers in the Dock District to pick up the slack.

### LADY SALHAR DOMINATES THE SECOND LABYRINTH
**The Coins** — Lady Anilah Salhar continues her astonishing winning streak at the Second Labyrinth. The wife of Osirion's Grand Ambassador has become a formidable player of the house game, *maze*, drawing large crowds and raising eyebrows among the district's elite. While her skills are undeniable, her frequent late-night victories have sparked rumors about her true alliances and whether she might be receiving *outside* assistance in navigating the complex wagers.
