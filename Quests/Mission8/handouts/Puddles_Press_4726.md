## HEADLINES

### CLINICS BURSTING AS MUCKRAKERS SCRAMBLE
**The Puddles** — The sudden surge of severe fever cases has pushed local almshouses past their breaking point. While uptown physicians issue warnings about the "children's disease," down here in the muck, rumors point to a different cause. Are the Muckrakers covering up a turf war, or are rogue alchemists testing cures on our destitute? Whatever the reason, unbadged carriages have been spotted spiriting the sick away in the dead of night. Stay vigilant, citizens!

### ANCIENT AZLANTI MOSAIC FOUND IN SEWERS
**Undercity** — Adula Tremane and her respected sewer crew stumbled upon an astonishing find yesterday: an intact, ancient Azlanti mosaic hidden behind a collapsed brick wall. Tremane, known for her strict self-discipline and honest work, immediately reported the find to the proper authorities, bypassing the corrupt elements of the Sewer Commission. The mosaic reportedly depicts a stylized map of the Starstone Isle, though scholars have yet to examine it closely.

### MARKET MASTER ANNAVI PROPOSES NEW BARQUE BAZAAR FEES
**The Puddles** — Tensions are running high at the Barque Bazaar after Market Master Annavi announced a proposed increase in mooring and stall fees. The proud Minatan merchant claims the funds are necessary to repair rotting docks and hire more guards, but local vendors are furious. "We're already bargaining with our lives," shouted one fishmonger. Annavi maintains her quick wit in the face of the backlash, but the political waters in The Puddles are growing increasingly choppy.

### THE GROGGY FROGGY ANNOUNCES ANNUAL CONTEST
**Westgate** — Good news for thirsty patrons! Annara Laskin, the tiefling sorcerer and proprietor of the Groggy Froggy, has officially announced the date for this year's "Froggy Grog" drinking contest. Thanks to Laskin's sorcerous upkeep, the tavern has completely recovered its reputation, and the event promises to be the biggest yet. Just remember: magic is strictly prohibited during the drinking bouts, and any participant who turns into a toad will be disqualified!
