*The City at the Center of the World — Evening Edition, 4726 AR*

## 🗞️ CITYWIDE HEADLINES

### TERRIFYING MAGICS AND CHAOS AT THE ANTLER LODGE!
**Eastgate** — Chaos erupted early yesterday at the **Antler Lodge**, a well-known temple of Erastil currently serving as a convalescent ward for victims of the ongoing "children's fever." Witnesses report that shortly after a visit from Pharasmin High Priest Morvan, a horrifying display of dark magic—a grotesque wall of living flesh and writhing tentacles—suddenly blocked the temple's corridors.

Panic spilled into the streets when a young girl’s voice was heard screaming that temple guards were "murdering children." While temple authorities and House Blakros sponsors have downplayed the incident, the Starwatch is currently investigating the bizarre disturbance and the whereabouts of several missing patients.

### ALARMS RING OUT AT THE NEW THASSILONIAN EMBASSY
**Foreign Quarter** — The usually pristine and heavily warded grounds of the **New Thassilonian Embassy** were thrown into high alert yesterday afternoon. Pedestrians reported seeing strange visual anomalies and thick, billowing black smoke erupting from within the consulate's walls.

Embassy guards were seen rushing the halls, though Lord Firandivar has officially declined to comment on the matter, citing internal diplomatic affairs. The incident has fueled ongoing speculation regarding the secretive nature of the Thassilonian mission here in the city.

### BRAZEN AMBUSH NEAR THE PATHFINDER SOCIETY
**Foreign Quarter** — In a shocking display of daylight violence, three masked and hooded assailants ambushed a group of young travelers just streets away from the Grand Lodge. The attackers, wielding returning poisoned throwing knives, engaged their targets briefly before vanishing in a cloud of thick alchemical smoke.

The Starwatch arrived too late to apprehend the culprits. While the victims seemingly escaped unharmed, citizens are urged to remain vigilant. The local watch has dismissed rumors connecting the attack to the Guild of Wonders.

## 🌆 LIFE IN THE CITY: 4726 AR

### PRECIPICE QUARTER RENOVATION NEARS COMPLETION
The long-awaited reconstruction of the Precipice Quarter is entering its final stages! Absalom's proudest civic project will soon see its ribbon-cutting ceremonies. While minor delays due to "subsidence" and the unearthing of deeper, older cellars continue to prompt the occasional rerouting of tours, Acting Primarch Wynsal Starborn has assured the public that the new quarter will be open by year's end.

### KORTOS REGATTA PREPARATIONS & A MYSTERIOUS RETURN
The Docks are humming with activity as preparations for the grand Kortos Regatta are officially underway. Thanks to Harbormaster Adrielle Neprathep’s continuing reforms, the harbor has never been busier. In a bizarre twist of fate, Captain Tanner’s legendary pearl trophy—infamously stolen in 4718—has mysteriously resurfaced at the starting line overnight!

### PUBLIC HEALTH ADVISORY: THE PUDDLES
City physicians are warning of an uptick in a severe fever spreading through the crowded slums of The Puddles. Meanwhile, the mysterious "children's disease" that leaves youths in a weakened, dream-like state continues to puzzle clerics across the city. Charitable wards and Primarch's-name almshouses are currently operating at maximum capacity.

### SCHOLARLY RENAISSANCE IN THE WISE QUARTER
Scion Lady Darchana has announced that the Arcanamirium's grand new wing will open to students next month. The expansion marks a new golden age of arcane commerce and study in Absalom. Meanwhile, the Blakros Museum has announced a temporary closure of its eastern exhibit following reports of newly acquired artifacts "rattling their cases."
