**A Joint Mandate for Order, Duty, and Prosperity in Kintargo**

By Order of Lord-Mayor **Barzillai Thrune** and in accordance with the principles of the **Eternal Vanguard**, let these proclamations be enacted. These decrees uphold the foundational tenets of the Eternal Vanguard's philosophy: **social welfare, hard work, and justice**. ## Proclamation the First: Civic Hygiene and Collective Effort
All slayers of city pests (defined as doves, mice, and ravens) who present said pests to the Dottari shall be rewarded with a bounty of **1 copper piece**. This mandate supports **Public Safety** and promotes the communal effort of keeping our urban environment clean as an act of duty.

## Proclamation the Second: Duty and Veneration
All places of public business must display in a position of prominence a portrait of **Her Exalted Majesty Queen Abrogail II** AND a portrait of the **Supreme Guide and Grand Arbiter, Elara Dawnbringer**, each measuring no less than 11 by 17 inches. This act demonstrates required **Duty** to lawful authority and upholds the Vanguard's principles of order.
* Affordable portraits are available for **5 gp each**.
* Failure to correct an error within one week results in a fine of **20 gp per week**.
* Persistent failure to pay fines results in the **seizure of the business**.

## Proclamation the Third: Resource Management and Utility
Anyone who captures, alive and unharmed, a feral dog of a weight exceeding 50 pounds is to be rewarded with a payment of **2 silver pieces** upon transfer of the dog to the Dottari. All resources must be managed efficiently for the greater **Wealth** and **Collective Prosperity**.

## Proclamation the Fourth: Equality in Public Appearance
The right to wear fine embroidered clothing in public is hereafter proscribed to anyone other than agents of House Thrune or the Eternal Vanguard. This measure reinforces the principle that all citizens are bound by the same **Codex** and discourages unnecessary displays of difference.
* Anyone caught wearing such clothing must turn it over and pay a fine equal to **10% the clothing's market value**.
* Failure to comply results in impoundment and imprisonment for **1 to 4 days**.

## Proclamation the Fifth: Stewardship of Collective Resources
**Grain is life!** Should grain be spilled in public, it must be gathered, cleaned, and repackaged within the hour. This decree enforces strict **Resource Management** to the benefit of all citizens.
* Any person who allows grain to go ungathered after a spillage shall be fined **1 copper piece per grain**.
* If no attempt is made to gather the grain, the penalty increases to **202 to 400 cp** per pound of grain spilled.
* Failure to pay fines results in **2 to 12 days** of incarceration.

## Proclamation the Sixth: Readiness for Duty
The imbibing of night tea brings a dangerous imbalance to the slumbering mind. This ensures the **Social Welfare** and readiness of the citizenry for **Hard Work** and unwavering **Duty**.
* Anyone caught drinking tea after curfew is fined **10 gp**.
* Failure to pay the fine results in **1 to 4 days** of incarceration.

## Proclamation the Seventh: Social Harmony and Cultural Alignment
The odor and flavor of mint is an abomination to the refined palate. This is a necessary measure to maintain the aesthetic standards of **Cultural Preservation** and **Social Harmony**.
* Being caught with mint confections incurs a **20 gp fine**.
* Failure to pay the fine results in **1 to 6 days** of incarceration.
* Mint consumed or used in other forms is not illegal.
