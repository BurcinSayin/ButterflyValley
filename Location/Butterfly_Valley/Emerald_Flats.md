# Emerald Flats: The Breadbasket of Butterfly Valley

## Overview

Emerald Flats is a peaceful, sprawling farming community situated near a large, crystalline lake in the Butterfly Valley demiplane. Located approximately one week of road travel to the northeast of Bastion City, it serves as the primary agricultural hub for the region, providing vital food supplies to Bastion City and other surrounding settlements.

## Geography

The community is nestled in a lush, flat basin surrounded by gently rolling hills that shield it from harsh weather. The climate is temperate and stable, ideal for year-round farming. The nearby lake provides an abundant source of freshwater for irrigation and fishing.

## Economy and Agriculture

The economy of Emerald Flats is entirely agrarian. Key products include:
- Wheat and other staple grains
- A wide variety of vegetables and fruits
- Freshwater fish from the nearby lake
- Livestock, primarily sheep and cattle

The community operates on a cooperative model, where farmers share resources, tools, and labor during peak planting and harvesting seasons.

## Culture and Society

The inhabitants of Emerald Flats are hardworking, pragmatic, and deeply connected to the land. The community is tight-knit, valuing mutual support and self-sufficiency. 

- **Festivals:** The most important events are the Spring Seeding and the Autumn Harvest festivals, featuring feasts, music, and friendly competitions.
- **Education:** Practical skills in agriculture, animal husbandry, and basic survival are prioritized.

## Defense and Security

Emerald Flats does not have a standing army. They rely on local militias composed of farmers and hunters, who are more than capable of handling minor threats from local wildlife. For significant dangers, they depend on support from Bastion City.
